Hogwarts

Hogwarts Castle is a building of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, regarded as the finest wizarding school in the world. It's a large, seven-storey high building supported by magic, with a hundred and forty two staircases throughout its many towers and turrets and very deep dungeons. The castle was built in the late Early Middle Ages (c. 993) by a wizard architect and the 4 most celebrated wizards of the age: Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw and Salazar Slytherin.

Camp Half-Blood

Camp Half-Blood is a Greek demigod training facility located on the north shore of Long Island. The camp is directed by the god Dionysus, who the campers call "Mr. D," and Chiron, a Centaur who is the activities director. The camp has magical borders which are enforced by the Golden Fleece, whose magical powers protecting the camp from monsters and invaders.
