Harry Potter: short description.

Harry Potter is a series of novels by J.K. Rowling. It is about a young boy named Harry Potter and his adventures as he attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, learns how to perform magic and comes face to face with his archenemy, Lord Voldemort.

Percy Jackson: short description.

Percy Jackson is a series of novels by Rick Riordan. It is about a young demigod (half mortal, half Greek god) and his adventures around the world. His life is filled with epic battles with gods and other mythological creatures.
